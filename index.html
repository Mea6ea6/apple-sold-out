FAQ

  • “What cities do you work in?”

    Our craftsmen and office work all over the world.

  • “How do I delete all data from my old phone?”

    Go to "Settings" > "General" > "Reset" > "Erase Content and Settings". This will delete all data from the device.

  • “Do you buy phones without FaceID?”

    Yes, we accept phones without Face ID, but the rating will be lower.

  • “Is it possible to exchange several gadgets for one?”

    Yes, you can exchange several devices for one gadget with or without additional payment, depending on the cost of the devices.

  • “Do you sell new iPhones?”

    Yes, we offer both new and refurbished iPhones.

  • “Where do you put the old equipment?”

    We either restore old equipment for resale, or send it for spare parts, or for disposal in accordance with environmental standards.
